01 — the problem

Agent payments have
one factor: the agent.

It signs alone. Trick it once — a hidden line on a page it reads — and the money's gone.
prompt injection · no human in the loop · no chargeback
02 — why today's fixes don't cut it

Everyone's second factor lives
where the attacker can reach it.

Cloud cosigners and intent-checkers exist — but the key that stops the payment sits online. Hack the server, and it's gone.
03 — airlock

The second factor runs offline.
On your phone.

No network — nothing to remotely hack. It reads the payment, signs only if it's safe, and the chain won't settle without it.
